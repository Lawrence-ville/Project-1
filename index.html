Male Moose
A moose is a large, herbivorous mammal found in northern forests. Characterized by its long legs, broad antlers in males, and distinctive dewlap or "bell" under its throat, it feeds on vegetation and is adapted to cold climates.

Female Moose
A female moose, or cow, is a large herbivore with long legs and a dewlap beneath her throat. She lacks the males' impressive antlers but is vital for reproduction, nurturing calves and foraging for vegetation in northern forests.

Elephant
An elephant is a large, intelligent mammal known for its trunk, tusks, and massive size. Found in Africa and Asia, they live in social herds, use their trunks for various tasks, and have complex behaviors and strong familial bonds.

CHEETAH
A cheetah is a sleek, fast cat renowned for its incredible speed, reaching up to 60-70 mph. Native to Africa, it has a lightweight body, distinctive black tear marks, and is adapted for short bursts of high-speed hunting.
